Our Story

Passion on every plate.

Velora Kitchen was born from a simple belief: dining should be an emotional experience. We blend the vibrant, nostalgic spices of Indian heritage with the precise execution of Continental culinary techniques.

Chef Aryan Mehta and his team curate the seasonal menu to ensure that every visit feels like a new discovery—intimate, luxurious, and deeply comforting.
